We use the collected data to:

3. Legal Basis for Processing

Under the GDPR, we rely on the following legal bases:

  • Consent (e.g. newsletter signup)
  • Legitimate interest (e.g. site analytics)
  • Legal obligation where applicable

4. Your Rights Under GDPR

  • Access your data
  • Request correction or deletion
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Object to processing in certain cases
  • Lodge a complaint with the Irish Data Protection Commission

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal data. We may share data with trusted service providers (e.g., newsletter platforms, Google Analytics) under strict data protection agreements.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this policy, please contact us at dataprivacy@k9gamestudios.com.
